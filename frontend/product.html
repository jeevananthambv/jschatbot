Self-Activated Heating Pad

Reusable sodium acetate-based heating pad that provides consistent, controlled heat for 4-6 hours without electricity or hot water.

  • Activates with a simple click
  • Maintains safe temperature (40-50°C)
  • Reusable up to 100 cycles
  • Washable, adjustable cover

Natural Herbal Sachets

Preservative-free herbal blend using traditional ingredients known for their anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties.

  • 100% natural ingredients
  • No chemical preservatives
  • Refrigeration not required
  • Easy to consume with warm water

Simple 3-Step Usage

Designed for easy use in school environments

1

Activate Heat

Click the metal disc in the heating pad to activate. Wait 30 seconds for heat generation.

2

Apply & Consume

Place heating pad on lower abdomen. Mix herbal sachet with warm water and drink.

3

Reuse & Recharge

After use, boil heating pad to reset. Reuse both components multiple times.
