Frequently Asked Questions

Are these resources really free to use?

Yes, all our educational materials are completely free to download and use. We believe in open access to menstrual health education. You can use, share, and adapt these materials for educational purposes.

Can I translate these materials into local languages?

Absolutely! We encourage translation and localization. If you translate our materials, please share them with us so we can make them available to others. We can also provide editable source files upon request.

How can I request custom materials for my organization?

We welcome requests for customized materials. Please contact our resource team at resources@thermarelief.org with details about your requirements, target audience, and intended use.

Do you provide physical copies of these materials?

For large-scale implementations with our partner organizations, we can arrange for printed materials. For individual requests, we recommend downloading and printing the PDF files locally to reduce costs and environmental impact.

How often are these resources updated?

We update our resources quarterly based on new research, user feedback, and field experiences. Subscribe to our newsletter to receive updates about new materials and revised versions.
