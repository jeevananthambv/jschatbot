The Global Crisis

Menstrual inequality affects education, health, and futures worldwide

Education Loss

23% absenteeism in India, 20% in Africa, 15% in Asia - Girls miss critical school days. Some never return.

Health Disparities

Lack of pain management, poor hygiene access, and untreated conditions like endometriosis affect futures globally.
